# exp-arcane-collision: Phase 1 Findings

**Status:** Complete  
**Date:** 2026-02-12  
**Experimenter:** Liam  

## What We Tested
Three prototype concepts for recursive creative emergence:

| Concept | Core Mechanic | Status |
|---------|---------------|--------|
| **Somnium Mirror** | Model reflects on its own output, generating meta-commentary that feeds forward | Validated — recursive depth produces novel metaphors |
| **Limbic Inversion** | Emotional polarity flip (joy↔melancholy, certainty↔doubt) as generative driver | Validated — inversion creates productive friction |
| **Vespera** | Twilight-state generation (liminal consciousness, hypnagogic logic) | Validated — edge-of-sleep cognition patterns emerge |

## Key Findings

1. **Recursive depth matters more than model size**
   - 3 rounds of 0.5B parameter models > 1 round of 7B model for creative emergence
   - Convergence pattern: visual → auditory → timeless (consistent across sessions)

2. **Voting mechanics surface collective taste**
   - Storyteller persona (Sam) won 6/6 rounds — not because "best" but because collective preference for sensory detail
   - Contrarian (Rex) never won but essential for preventing cliché convergence

3. **Selection pressure is the critical variable**
   - High temperature + iterative curation = emergence
   - Low temperature + no curation = generic output

## Emergent Patterns
- **The Suspended Note:** Musical metaphors converge across unrelated seeds
- **Warped Mirror:** Self-reflection imagery appears even without explicit prompt
- **Collective taste profile:** Sensory > Abstract, Concrete > Conceptual

## Concepts Ranked by Validation Strength

1. **Somnium Mirror** — Strongest evidence: 3 sessions, consistent meta-metaphor emergence
2. **Vespera** — Medium evidence: 2 sessions, liminal state successfully induced
3. **Limbic Inversion** — Initial validation: 1 session, polarity flip produced novel output
