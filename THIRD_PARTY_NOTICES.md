### Files derived from NODEPROMPT

- `src/nodeprompt/types/` — Type definitions (node.ts, edge.ts, extraction.ts)
- `src/nodeprompt/layout/` — Spatial layout algorithms (coordinates.ts, fibonacciSphere.ts, SphereLayout.ts)
- `src/nodeprompt/extraction/` — Extraction schemas and budget allocation (schemas.ts)
- `src/nodeprompt/synthesis/` — Prompt synthesis (PromptSynthesizer.ts)
- `src/nodeprompt/store/` — Graph and history state management (GraphStore.ts, HistoryStore.ts)
- `src/nodeprompt/gesture/` — Hand gesture engine (GestureEngine.ts, gestureTypes.ts)
